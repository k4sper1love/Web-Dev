Uzbek pilaf

  • Time: 2 hours
  • Serves: 5
Ingredients Quantity
Mutton 1 kg
Carrots 4 pcs
Garlic 2 heads
Red pepper 2 pcs
Cumin 1 tbsp
Barberry 1 tbsp
Coriander 1 tsp
Salt

Step 1: Rinse the rice thoroughly several times so that the last water is clear after rinsing.

Step 2: Remove the top husks from the garlic heads, but leave them whole, do not divide into cloves.

Step 3: Peel 3 onions and cut the onion into half rings.

Step 4: Peel the carrots and cut them into thin strips.

Step 5: Heat a cast-iron cauldron and heat the vegetable oil in it. Add 1 onion to the oil without peeling it. Take it out only when it turns black.

Step 6: Put the chopped onion in the cauldron and fry it until golden brown.

Step 7: Wash the lamb and cut into medium-sized cubes. Add to the onion and fry until golden brown. Add the carrots and fry them with meat and onions, without stirring, for 3 minutes. After that, mix the ingredients and cook for another 10 minutes.

Step 8: Pour boiling water into the cauldron. It should be 1 cm higher than the contents of the cauldron. Add hot red pepper, turn down the heat and simmer for about 1 hour.

Step 9: Mix the ground cumin with coriander and barberry and add the spices to the meat. Add salt, turn down the heat and cook until the carrots are soft (10-15 minutes).

Step 10: Rinse the rice again and put it on the meat, smooth it out. Increase the heat to maximum and pour boiling water over it. The water level should be 3 cm above the contents.

Step 11: When the rice has completely absorbed the water, press the garlic heads into it. Then, in the pilaf, it is necessary to make several through punctures with a thin wooden skewer.

Step 12: Cover the pilaf with a lid, make a minimum heat and simmer until the rice is ready for another half hour.

Fried potatoes with onions

  • Time: 30 minuites
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Potatoes 760 g
Garlic 1 clove
Vegetable oil 3-4 tablespoons
Onion 180 g
Salt

Step 1: Peel, wash and dry the potatoes using a paper or clean kitchen towel. The potatoes should be completely dry.

Step 2: Cut the potatoes into medium-sized cubes.

Step 3: Peel 3 onions and cut the onion into half rings.

Step 4: Heat the vegetable oil well in a frying pan over high heat. Put the sliced potatoes in a frying pan and smooth them out. Fry the potatoes for about 4-5 minutes, without stirring, until the underside of the potato cubes is browned.

Step 5:Stir and fry for another 4-5 minutes.

Step 6: Stir again. Now the potatoes will have a more or less uniform golden color.

Step 7: Peel the onion and cut into quarter rings.

Step 8: Add the onion to the potatoes. Reduce the heat to medium.

Step 9: Now fry the potatoes with onions, stirring every minute, until the onions are soft and browned, and the potato pieces will not break easily when pressed.

Step 10: Add salt. Peel the garlic and squeeze it through a press into a frying pan (this is optional). Mix it up.

Step 11: Turn down the heat to a minimum and cook the potatoes for about 3 more minutes, until the garlic reveals its flavor.
